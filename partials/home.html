The Value of Money

Before we can talk about the value of money, we must first define money. What is money? According to Investopia, money is any item or medium of exchange that is accepted by people for the payment of goods and services.
The value of money is determined by the demand or need for it. The value for money can be determined by what good or service an indivdual will exchange it for. when talking about the value of money it is impossible to not mention terms like inflation and deflation. Inflation is the period when the value of money decreases over time and deflation is when the value of money increases over time. To read more on the value of money, read the article by Kimberly Amadeo titled "What Gives Money Its Value?".

GMO Foods

GMO stands for genetically modified organism, which is also known as Genetically modified foods. These are foods "produced from organisms that have had changes introduced into their DNA using various methods of genetic engineering. Genetic engineering techniques allow for the introduction of new traits as well as greater control over traits when compared to previous methods, such as selective breeding and mutation breeding." (Wikipedia).
